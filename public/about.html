About kroma

Last updated: 2026-07-01

kroma is an AI ecommerce image workspace for sellers and creative teams that need product main images, white-background images, detail-page visuals, and related ecommerce marketing assets.

What We Provide

Users upload product photos, choose ecommerce modules, and generate images for listing, advertising, product display, and detail-page use.

Who Uses kroma

kroma is built for ecommerce sellers, brand operators, content teams, and agencies that create product visuals on a recurring basis.

kroma Pricing

Last updated: 2026-07-01

kroma sells paid credits and subscription plans for AI ecommerce image generation. Users upload product photos, choose modules, and spend credits to generate product images, white-background images, AI tool edits, and fashion detail-page visuals.

One-time credit packs

One-time credit packs are not auto-renewing. Credits are delivered to the user's kroma account after successful payment confirmation.

Credit delivery and usage

Credits are delivered to the user's kroma account after payment confirmation. Credit cost is shown before generation and depends on selected feature, module count, quantity, resolution, and brand version options. Failed generation tasks do not consume credits.
